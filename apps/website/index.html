The idea behind the mark

The mark says everything. Two M-arches, split by a line. The upper arch is you — present, full, in charge. The lower arch is your miniMe, working quietly below the surface.

Most founders hit a wall. Not because their ideas are small, but because there’s only one of them. Every email, every follow-up, every decision that could have been made from context they’ve already given a dozen times.

MiniMe changes that ratio. Feed it your voice and your judgment. Point it at the work. It operates in your image while you do the things only you can do.
